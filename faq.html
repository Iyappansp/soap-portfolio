📦 Ordering & MOQ

What is your minimum order quantity?+
MOQs vary by product: Handmade Soaps — 100 units per variant. Bath Bombs — 50 units per variant. Gift Sets — 24 sets. Private Label — 200 units. You can mix and match products across categories to reach the minimum. Volume discounts kick in at 500+ units.
Can I order samples before placing a bulk order?+
Absolutely. We offer a curated wholesale sample pack of 10 products for £35 including standard UK shipping (£55 international). This amount is fully credited against your first bulk order. Custom sample sets for specific product ranges or scents are available — contact us for pricing.
How do I apply for a wholesale account?+
Complete the wholesale application form on our Wholesale Program page. We review all applications within 48 business hours. Approved partners receive a welcome email with their trade pricing catalogue, account login, and dedicated account manager contact details.
Can I mix different scents or variants within a product order?+
Yes. For soaps and bath bombs, you can mix variants as long as the total order meets the per-category MOQ. For example, a soap order of 100 units could be split across 4 different scents at 25 units each. Private label orders require a single variant per minimum batch.
How do I reorder once my wholesale account is set up?+
Approved partners have access to an account portal for easy reordering. You can also reorder directly by email to your account manager or by phone. For regular clients, we offer subscription supply with scheduled monthly or quarterly deliveries.

🧼 Products & Ingredients

Are your products genuinely natural? No synthetics at all?+
All our products are made with natural, plant-based ingredients. We use no synthetic fragrances (only pure essential oils), no SLS or SLES, no parabens, no artificial preservatives, and no petroleum derivatives. Full ingredient lists are provided for every product on request. Our core soap range carries COSMOS Natural certification — the gold standard for natural cosmetics verification.
How long is the shelf life of your soaps and bath bombs?+
Soaps: 18–24 months from date of manufacture when stored in cool, dry conditions away from direct sunlight. Bath Bombs: 12–18 months. We provide best-before dates on all products and recommend FIFO (first in, first out) stock rotation. All products shipped are at minimum 12 months from their best-before date.
Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?+
Yes. All our formulations are dermatologist-tested and pH-balanced (7.5–9 for soaps). We avoid the most common skin irritants. Our Goat Milk, Colloidal Oatmeal, and Organic Shea ranges are specifically formulated for sensitive, eczema-prone, and allergy-prone skin. Always recommend patch-testing for individuals with known sensitivities.
Are your bath bombs safe for children?+
Our Kids Collection bath bombs are specifically formulated for children aged 3 and above — gentle, low-fragrance formulas with skin-safe colorants. Our standard adult bath bomb range uses stronger essential oil concentrations and is recommended for ages 12+. Age guidance is on all packaging.
Are your products vegan?+
The majority of our range is vegan. Exceptions are clearly marked: our Goat Milk Soap range contains goat milk, and some products contain beeswax or raw honey. Our full product catalogue indicates vegan status for every product. All products are cruelty-free (Leaping Bunny certified).

🏷️ Private Label

What's the difference between white label and private label?+
White label: our existing formulations with your branding applied. Starts from 100 units, 3-week lead time. Private label: custom formulations developed specifically for your brand — your scent, your ingredient preferences, your unique product. Starts from 200 units, 4-week lead time from sample approval. Both include label design support.
How long does private label development take?+
After the discovery call, sample development takes 7–10 days. Once you approve the samples, packaging artwork takes 5–7 days. Production and dispatch is typically 2–3 weeks after artwork approval. Total from first contact to delivered product: usually 6–8 weeks for first-time orders. Reorders are much faster — typically 3–4 weeks.
Can I use my own packaging design?+
Yes. If you have existing brand assets and packaging design, our team will adapt and apply them. Alternatively, our in-house designers can create your packaging from scratch — this is included in the private label service at no additional cost. We just need your brand guidelines and any preferences.
Is my formula kept confidential and exclusive to my brand?+
Absolutely. All custom formulations developed for your brand are held under a confidentiality agreement and are not offered to other clients. Your scent formula is registered to your brand account. We sign NDAs upon request before any development begins.

🚚 Shipping & Delivery

Which countries do you ship to?+
We currently ship to 80+ countries worldwide via DHL Express, FedEx International, and international freight for large orders. Some cosmetic ingredients have export restrictions in certain territories — contact us to confirm availability for your specific country.
How long does delivery take?+
UK: 2–3 business days. Europe: 3–5 business days. USA/Canada: 5–7 business days. Asia Pacific: 5–8 business days. Rest of world: 7–14 business days. All timelines from dispatch, not from order placement. Express options available for most destinations.
Is shipping insured? What happens if my order arrives damaged?+
All wholesale orders are fully insured for their invoice value. If products arrive damaged, photograph the damage immediately, retain all packaging, and notify us within 48 hours of receipt. We will arrange replacement shipment or credit at no cost to you.
Do you provide customs documentation for international orders?+
Yes. We provide comprehensive export documentation including commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, safety data sheets (MSDS), and cosmetic product notifications where required. Our logistics team handles all paperwork — you just receive the goods.

✅ Certifications & Safety

What certifications do your products hold?+
COSMOS Natural (core soap range), Leaping Bunny Cruelty-Free (all products), ISO 22716 GMP (manufacturing facility), CE-marked cosmetics (EU compliance), Dermatologist tested (all formulations). We also hold Fair Trade verification for our shea butter and cocoa butter supply chains. All certificates available on request.
Are your products compliant with EU cosmetic regulations?+
Yes. All products sold into the EU have CPNP (Cosmetic Products Notification Portal) notification, safety assessments by a qualified cosmetic chemist, and full INCI ingredient labelling. We provide all compliance documentation to EU-based wholesale partners.
Can I use your certifications in my marketing materials?+
Wholesale partners may reference that products are "made with COSMOS Natural certified ingredients" and "cruelty-free (Leaping Bunny)" in their marketing. The COSMOS and Leaping Bunny logos can only be used by certified brand owners — for private label clients, we can discuss certification transfer as part of your programme.

💳 Payments & Terms

What payment methods do you accept?+
We accept bank transfer (BACS/SWIFT/SEPA), major credit and debit cards, and PayPal for smaller orders. For established wholesale accounts, we offer 30-day payment terms (subject to credit check). First orders are typically prepayment or 50% deposit/50% on dispatch.
What currency do you invoice in?+
We invoice in GBP (£), EUR (€), and USD ($). Your account manager will set your preferred currency when your account is created. Prices are fixed in your chosen currency for 12 months at approval date.
What is your returns and refunds policy?+
We stand behind every product. If products don't meet the agreed specification, we will replace or refund. Please note: as natural products, minor batch-to-batch variation in colour and texture is normal and not grounds for return. Claims based on product defect or incorrect order must be raised within 14 days of receipt.
