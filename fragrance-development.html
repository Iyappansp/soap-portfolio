Custom Development

How We Build Your Signature Scent

1

Scent Briefing

We start with a detailed scent brief. You describe the mood, target customer, product occasion, and reference scents you love. We translate this into fragrance direction.

2

Blend Formulation

Our perfumers create 3 unique blends using pure essential oils and botanical extracts. Each formula is documented for full replicability across production runs.

3

Sample & Feedback

Scent samples shipped within 7 days. You test, share with your team and customers, then send feedback. We refine until it's perfect — unlimited revisions included.

4

Formula Lock & Scale

Once approved, your formula is locked and registered to your brand. We can scale from 200 to 50,000+ units with guaranteed scent consistency every batch.
