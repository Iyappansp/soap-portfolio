Our Beginning

Started with a Pot, a Purpose, and Pure Ingredients

Naturelle Supply Co was founded in 2010 by Sarah Chen and Marcus Webb — a biochemist and a packaging designer who shared one frustration: why were mainstream soaps filled with chemicals that stripped skin dry?

They started cold-pressing soaps in a small workshop, gifting bars to friends and local boutiques. Within two years, hotels were calling. By 2015, they were shipping internationally. Today, Naturelle supplies over 580 businesses across 32 countries — every product still made with the same uncompromising natural standards as that first batch.

Our Journey

14 Years of Growth

From a two-person workshop to an international wholesale operation — built on quality, never compromising on ingredients.

2010

The First Batch

Sarah and Marcus hand-press their first 200 rose petal soap bars in a rented workshop. Gifted to 12 local boutiques — all sell out in a week.

2012

First Hotel Contract

The Harrington Hotel Group places a 5,000-unit order for custom amenity soaps. First large-scale production run completed in 4 weeks.

2014

Bath Bomb Range Launched

Our now-bestselling bath bomb collection introduced. 6 original scents — Rose, Ocean, Lavender, Citrus, Charcoal, and Vanilla. All still available today.

2016

Private Label Programme

Launched full private label service after 40+ clients asked for branded products. First year: 28 brands launched using our formulations.

2019

COSMOS Certification

Achieved COSMOS Natural certification for our core soap range. Became first UK wholesale soap supplier with full supply chain transparency documentation.

2022

Zero Plastic Milestone

Achieved zero plastic packaging across all product lines. Switched entirely to kraft paper, glass, and compostable materials.

2026

580+ Partners Globally

Supplying 580+ wholesale partners across 32 countries. New fragrance studio opened. Custom scent development now available to all partners.

Mission & Values

Every material we use, every supplier we work with, every packaging decision is made with the planet's health as a core consideration.
