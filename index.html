FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our wholesale program and ordering process.

View All FAQs
What is the minimum order quantity?+
Our minimum order quantity varies by product: Soaps start from 100 units per variant, Bath Bombs from 50 units, and Gift Sets from 24 sets. Private label orders start from 200 units. Volume discounts apply from 500+ units.
How long does production and delivery take?+
Standard stock items ship within 5–7 business days. Custom formulations and private label orders typically take 3–4 weeks from sample approval. We offer express production for an additional fee.
Do you offer product samples before bulk orders?+
Absolutely. We offer a curated sample pack (10 products) for $45 including shipping, credited toward your first bulk order. Custom formulation samples are quoted separately.
Are your products certified organic or natural?+
All our products are made with natural, plant-based ingredients with no synthetic fragrances, SLS, or parabens. Select lines carry COSMOS Natural certification. Full ingredient lists and certificates available upon request.
