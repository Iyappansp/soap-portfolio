All FAQs
Can I get a sample pack before placing a bulk order?+
Yes! We offer a curated wholesale sample pack featuring 10 of our best-selling products for $45 (including shipping), fully credited to your first order. Custom scent samples are available on request with a development fee.
What certifications do your products carry?+
Our products are dermatologist-tested, cruelty-free certified (Leaping Bunny), and a select range carries COSMOS Natural certification. All are free from SLS, parabens, synthetic fragrances, and artificial preservatives.
Do you offer white label in addition to private label?+
Absolutely. White label (our formulations, your label) starts from 100 units. Private label (custom formulations + custom branding) from 200 units. Both include design support from our in-house packaging team.
How do I set up a wholesale account?+
Submit the wholesale application form on our Wholesale Program page. We review all applications within 48 hours. Approved partners receive a dedicated account portal, trade pricing catalog, and access to our full product range.
