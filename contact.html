Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

How fast do you respond to wholesale enquiries?+
All wholesale and business enquiries receive a response within 24 business hours. Urgent enquiries can be flagged by phone — we'll reply the same day.
Do you offer a sample service before bulk orders?+
Yes. We offer a curated 10-product sample pack for £35 (credited to your first order). Custom sample sets for specific product ranges are also available on request.
Can I visit your facility?+
We welcome trade visits to our London facility by appointment. Contact us to schedule a factory tour — typically available Tuesday–Thursday mornings.
Do you ship internationally?+
We ship to 80+ countries via DHL Express and international freight for large orders. Full customs documentation, certificates of origin, and MSDS provided for all international shipments.
